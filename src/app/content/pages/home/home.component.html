About Us

Welcome to Blue Cross

Blue Cross Hospital is a private enterprise that provides health care services. It is centrally located in the heart of the capital, in Tripureshwor, Kathmandu Valley. Its motto is "Participating in Nation's Health With Safe, Standard and Satsifying Services" and this is to be achieved through the provision of modern, new, hi-tech diagnostic and treatment facilities services, health services that are not available or inadequately available in the country.

With this pledge, BCH was established in 1991 by a group of young entrepreneurs by setting up a Conventional Computerized Tomography (CT) scan, first from the private sector, and then gradually developed into a Polyclinic and Diagnostic Centre with Path lab, X ray, Ultrasound, Cardiological and neurological investigations and meanwhile adding Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) , Lithotripsy, Haemodialysis and so on. It further started in-patient services and officially registered as Blue Cross Hospital in 2010 with 25 beds (General beds, cabins), ICU with ventilator , modern OT with laparoscopic and Endourologic and Orthopedic surgical facilities and Emergency service. So it is multi-speciality , tertiary level health care centre. At present Blue Cross is a 50 bedded Multi-Speciality hospital.

CURATIVE

Curative medicine is the health care given for medical conditions where a cure is considered achievable. We use a comprehensive modality consisting of Medical Oncology, Surgical Oncology and Radiation Oncology to treat cancerous diseases.

DIAGNOSTIC

Medical diagnosis is the process of determining which disease or condition explains a person's symptoms and signs. We have the latest diagnostic facilities in our Laboratories and Imaging Departments to detect cancers in their earliest stages.
